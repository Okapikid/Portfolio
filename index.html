About Me

My name is Mike Augustine, but I'm Auggie to everyone who knows me (and @Okapikid everywhere online). I've spent the last decade in the design world in editing, writing, trends, and strategy. Having received both undergraduate (SIUE) and graduate (University of Colorado Denver) degrees in English Language and Literature, I'm a life-long learner with world-class problem-solving skills.


As a programmer, I'm interested in furthering my game-design skills. I have been a designer for over 5 years and a playtester for much longer. In January I launched the newest iteration of my alternate-reality game CXD through a vending machine in Cincinnati, OH, and hope to expand across the midwest.


In my free time I'm obsessed with puzzles and games of all kinds. I was the winningest Red Bull Mindgamer throughout the program's tenure, winning their Quantum Challenge (and experiencing a 2-week internship at the Vienna Center for Quantum Research), my team placed 10th in the world at the Red Bull Escape Room World Championships, I won their Memory challenge, and helped design, facilitate, and participated in their Idea Lab in collaboration with MIT. In the last two years I've won a handful of treasure hunts, including the $25K Mission treasure hunt.
